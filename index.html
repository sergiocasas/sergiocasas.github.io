About me

I’m a Senior Researcher at Waabi. Here, I apply my own research to the development of self-driving vehicle technology, focusing on autonomy algorithms ranging from perception to motion planning.

I am also a PhD student at the University of Toronto supervised by Professor Raquel Urtasun, and a member of the Machine Learning Group and the Vector Institute.
